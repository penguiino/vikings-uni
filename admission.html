Undergraduate Admissions for International Students


In order to apply as an international undergraduate student, first of all, the requirement stated below within the scope of the Turkish Higher Education Consulate legislation must be met and an online application must be submitted in accordance with the application conditions determined by viking University for the relevant year. International Students who are in the last year of high school or graduated from a school equivalent to Turkish high schools may apply to undergraduate programs offered at viking University.

1.APPLICATION

A. Before online application

You will be asked to upload the documents stated in the application requirements below, so you need to have scanned versions of each of these ready for upload. You will not be able to submit your application until all documents have been uploaded and all required fields have been filled in. Complete applications are reviewed on a continuous basis and you will be informed via email.

After filling and submitting the online application form, the applicants are directed to the online application fee payment page.

A 30 USD application fee needs to be paid in order for the application to be valid and evaluated. You can quickly pay with your Visa or Mastercard credit card from the application fee payment page. The application fee is paid only once for the relevant academic year and it is not refundable under any circumstances. The applications without application fee will not be valid and will not be evaluated.

B. Application Dates

Applications must be submitted by uploading all required documents to the online application form between the application dates stated below.

First Day of Application Period : January 18, 2022

Last Day for Applications: July 29, 2022 (Online application form will be closed at 16:00 with Turkish time.)

C. Who Can Apply?

Students who have graduated from an institution equivalent to Turkish high schools and those who are on their last year at high school may apply to the undergraduate programs, if they meet the application criteria below.

According to the Turkish Higher Education Council’s last legislation, applicants who quit Turkish citizenship and obtain a Blue Card, Turkish Republic of Northern Cyprus citizens and international students (except who enroll Embassy Schools and International Private Education Institutions in the MOBIS system) who enroll in secondary schools in Turkey starting from the 2022-2023 Academic Year will only be able to enter Higher Education Institutions with the Higher Education Institutions Exam (YKS) result.

2.ADMISSION

Applications will be evaluated by the relevant Faculty Administrative Board. Applicants are informed via e-mail about the progress of the admission process within 4-5 weeks after the online application is submitted. Please do not e-mail the university to ask about the admission decision.
